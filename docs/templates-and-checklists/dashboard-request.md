# Dashboard Feature Request (PRD-lite)

Use this template when stakeholders want new Superset dashboards or material changes to existing ones. Keep language clear for non-technical reviewers and link to existing assets when available.

---

## Request Summary

- **Requester and date**:
- **Dashboard or domain**:
- **Target audience** (moderation / community / engineering / leadership):
- **Phase goal** (minimum / operational / acceptable / exceptional):

---

## Problem or Opportunity

- What decision or workflow is blocked today?
- What outcome are we trying to improve for SFG members?

---

## Goals and Success Metrics

- Primary KPI(s) and target direction (up/down), including time zone (default UTC) and grain.
- Guardrail metrics (latency, error rates, member impact).

---

## Must-Have Requirements

- User stories, filters, and drill paths that must ship in the first release.
- Data constraints (freshness, retention windows, sensitivity).

---

## Nice-to-Have / Future

- Enhancements that can follow after launch without blocking the first release.

---

## Data Scope

- Source systems and tables (e.g., Discord Gateway events, warehouse tables).
- Required joins, segments, or feature flags to include/exclude.

---

## Visual and UX Expectations

- Chart types or layout preferences; any accessibility considerations (contrast, labeling).
- Default time range and segment filters.

---

## Access and Privacy

- Roles that should have access; PII or sensitive fields to aggregate or suppress.

---

## Validation and Acceptance Criteria

- Checks against source systems (e.g., Grafana logs, warehouse queries) with expected tolerances.
- Performance expectations for key charts (e.g., load time thresholds).

---

## Dependencies and Risks

- Upstream data readiness, product changes, or approvals needed.
- Known gaps or assumptions that could change scope.

---

## Rollout and Communication

- Links to dashboards, design mocks, or Slack/Discord threads.
- Reviewer list and sign-off approach.

---

## Change Log

- Date, update summary, and reviewer.
